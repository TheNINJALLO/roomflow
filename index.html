Help & Guided Tour

Welcome to RoomFlow Guided Estimator. This app helps you quickly capture room shapes, problems, and generate professional estimates in the field.

Guided Workflow Overview

  1. Job Info: Enter property and contact details (autosaved).
  2. Work Area: Select the foundation levels (Basement, Crawl Space, etc.).
  3. Add/Measure Rooms: Enter sizes manually, pick presets, draw custom shapes, or scan with camera.
  4. Work Needed: Toggle issues like water leaks, bowed walls, mold, or vapor barriers.
  5. Equipment: Configure pumps and dehumidifiers.
  6. Review: Verify measurements and check for missing info warnings.
  7. Costs & Margin: Inspect materials list and set margins (private view).
  8. Customer Proposal: Customize scope items for proposal generation.
  9. Save / Export: Download proposal PDFs or share links.

Troubleshooting Camera Scan

If AR/LiDAR scanning fails or permission is denied, tap More → Diagnostics to verify device status. You can fallback to manual measurement entries anytime without losing your job data.
